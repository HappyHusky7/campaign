About Campaign

Dive deeper into the story, the music, and the world of our show.

The Story

In the high-stakes arena of a presidential election, two brilliant strategists find themselves on opposite sides. One is a seasoned idealist, the other a ruthless pragmatist. As they battle for the hearts and minds of the nation, their professional rivalry ignites a passionate, secret affair that could derail both of their careers and the fate of the country. Campaign explores the cost of ambition, the nature of truth, and whether love can survive in a world where winning is everything.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the running time of the show?

The show runs approximately 2 hours and 30 minutes, including one 15-minute intermission.

Is the show suitable for all ages?

We recommend "Campaign" for audiences aged 13 and up due to mature themes and strong language.

Where is the theatre located?

The show is playing at the Grand Theatre, 123 Main Street, Theatre District.

Are there any strobe lighting or haze effects?

Yes, the production uses strobe lighting and theatrical haze. Please contact the box office if you have concerns about photosensitivity.
